What does telemedicine mean?

What does telemedicine mean?

Telemedicine refers to the use of technology to provide clinical healthcare remotely using video calls, phone calls, and other electronic communication methods.

What is telemedicine used for?

Telemedicine is used to consult doctors, monitor patients, provide follow-up visits, and manage chronic diseases remotely.

What equipment do you require for telemedicine?

For telemedicine, you need a smartphone, tablet, or computer with internet access and a webcam or camera for video calls.

What are the pros or advantages of telemedicine?

Key advantages include convenience, time savings, fewer travel needs, and increased access to specialists regardless of location.
